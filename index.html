About ART Mesonet Mumbai

The ART Mesonet Mumbai is a state-of-the-art high-resolution rainfall monitoring network. By deploying a dense grid of Automatic Rain Gauges (ARG) and Doppler X-Band Radar, we provide real-time urban weather monitoring to enhance flood preparedness and meteorological research for the Mumbai Metropolitan Region.

Project Infrastructure

Our network consists of the following critical components:

  • Automatic Rain Gauges (Tipping Bucket): High-precision measurement of rainfall intensity.
  • Doppler X-Band Radar: Advanced spatial tracking of storm cells.
  • Real-Time Data Logging: Continuous data transmission for immediate analysis.

Doppler X-Band Radar
