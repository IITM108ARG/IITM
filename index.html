ART Mesonet Mumbai

Description

ART Mesonet Mumbai is a dense urban observation network designed to provide real-time rainfall monitoring, storm tracking, and decision support for disaster management and scientific research.

Key Components

  • Automatic Rain Gauge Network (ARG)
  • Doppler X-Band Weather Radar
  • Real-time telemetry & data servers
  • Web-based rainfall visualization portal

Objectives

  • Improve urban flood early warning
  • Monitor extreme rainfall events
  • Support mesoscale weather research
  • Provide real-time data to civic authorities
